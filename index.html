ABOUT

PREFACE
Y2K KHAYALETHU TRADING was established in 2007 and formalized in 2008, with its objective to create jobs for the unemployed.

BACKGROUND
Since its inception in 2007. Y2K KHAYALETHU TRADING offers specialized services in the cleaning industry and has employed 15 people. Y2K KHAYALETHU TRADING operates Bloemfontein and also in the rest of the Free State and Northern Cape. The head office is located in Bloemfontein and all projects are co-ordinated from there.

VISON
Y2K KHAYALETHU TRADING, clean and deep clean your way into the future.

MISSION
Our mission is: "To build and maintain a business that reflects trust, quality, integrity and common values for our clients and staff to ensure long sustainable relations and results."

It is also our mission to:
  • Improve the standard of living amongst the disadvantaged communities by
    prompt delivery of quality service.
  • Provide an effective and efficient service to the community.

Also as part of the mission of Y2K KHAYALETHU TRADING, is to capacitate and create
employment to the previously disadvantaged, especially women and youth. Skills development
will play an important role in this whole process.

OUR VALUES
